Shahi Paneer

Shahi Paneer is a rich and creamy North Indian dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a flavorful tomato-based gravy with aromatic spices. It is often served with naan or rice.

Ingredients

  • 100g paneer, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped or grinded
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit or chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 tsp salt after tomato
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tsp coriander powder
  • 50g cheese
  • some coriander leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add onions, green chilies, and ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Sauté until the onions turn golden brown.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy.
  4. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Add the chopped paneer and cook for a few minutes until it absorbs the flavors.
  6. Add garam masala and mix well.
  7. Garnish with grated cheese and serve hot with naan or rice.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  9. Serve and enjoy your delicious Shahi Paneer!
